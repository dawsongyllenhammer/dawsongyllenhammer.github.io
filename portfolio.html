University Projects



The content below shows various websites that I have made as finals for classes at Western Washington University. Most of these websites are configured primarily for desktop and will likely look broken on other devices.


Title: Bellingham Hotel

Description: I created this website as an assignment for my CS202 class at WWU, which is a website for a fake hotel. The website itself is configured using SCSS, Javascript, and JQuery libraries. It's definitely one of my most complex websites, apart from the one your on of course.

Title: Rigor Mortis; A Composition of Corpses

Description: This is a rather morbid website I made as a project for my Honors 104 class at WWU. In this website I examined the concept of death as public knowledge, or the idea of being able to honestly adress the literal, physical sides of dying. It also includes 3 digital paintings I made based off of references of dead bodies. Beware of triggering content and whatnot.



Title: Generalizing the Irrational

Description: This is a little website/game I made for my Math 160 class about Phi, or the golden rectangle. It's about subdividing a rectange using the golden ratio. It's simple but interactive, and I had a lot of fun making it.
