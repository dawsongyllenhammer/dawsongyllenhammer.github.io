WHO AM I?

ABOUT

Hello! My name is Dawson and i'm a digital and traditional artist based in Bellingham, Washington. I am also studying Design, Web Design, and Web Development as a student at Western Washington University.

When it comes to my work, I consider my artwork to be somewhere in between Surrealism and Psychadellia. I also have an obsession with symbols. I realize that every visual element has some sort of contextual weight to it, And I like to place different symbols sets within a composition to see how meaning is distorted by them.

Some of my work is simply technical studies, but with my more inventive pieces, my creative process comes from mostly my subconcious. I rarely ever have a meaning, theme, or sketch planned out before I paint. Instead, I choose symbols at random that I feel will fit the composition, and as a result my ideation is somewhat unpredictable.

For any questions about my work, or if your interested in a commission, feel free to visit my Contact page.
