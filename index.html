About

Hello! My name is Dawson and i'm a digital and traditional artist based in Bellingham, Washington. I am currently studying Design, Web Design, and Front-End Web Development as a student at Western Washington University.


When it comes to my work, I consider my artwork to meddle somewhere in between Surrealism and Psychadellia. I also have an obsession with symbols. I realize that every visual element has some sort of contextual weight to it, And I like to place different symbols sets within a composition to see how meaning is distorted by them. I'm a huge fan of symbols like cigarettes, lighters, sculptures, crosses, revolvers, moons and knives.

Some of my work is really just technical studies, but with my more creative pieces, my process seems to come from mostly my subconcious. I rarely ever have a meaning, theme, or sketch planned out before I paint. Instead, I choose symbols at random that I feel will fit the composition, and as a result my ideation is pretty unpredictable, even to me. I like it this way though; Its like an emotional roller coaster with everything I paint.

I've also found that my interests change pretty vastly over time. my current goal has been to develop my paintings more fully, and really go in on the details. I want to end up with a composition thats more than just portrait + random object. But its gonna take a while before I get there.

If your interested in a commission, or you just wanna talk about the creative process, feel free to visit my Contact page (or just E-mail Me)
