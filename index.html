About

Hello! My name is Dawson and i'm a digital and traditional artist based in Bellingham, Washington. I am currently studying Design, Web Design, and Front-End Web Development as a student at Western Washington University.


My art has a lot to do with automatism, or the practice of production without a clear end goal set in mind. For my more imaginative works (that is, work that goes beyond the confines of “practice”,) my process for generating a drawing or painting is very chaotic and random, in an attempt to genuinely surprise myself with the end product. I almost always start by working from reference (or occasionally from life), but I attempt to go beyond the confines of the reference photo at some point. Sometimes I land a few meters away, sometimes I’m miles away from my source material. As a result, I guess my more specific goal is automatic realism, as well as automatic surrealism and automatic psychedelia.

My subject matter has primarily been the human form, but I’ve been trying to expand beyond that, moving more from the entity to the spatial environment. I do have an addiction to the application of technical skills, so I try my best not to cut corners unless my ego takes over.

I’d be flattered if my art disturbed you, amused you, confused you, or left you completely disinterested. I frankly don’t care what you take from it, but whatever your reaction, just know its welcome. Also, don’t smoke kids.

If your interested in a commission, or you just wanna talk about the creative process, feel free to visit my Contact page (or just E-mail Me)
